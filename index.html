How to use the planner

  1. Every standard table always shows all 10 seats. Table 7 remains a fixed joined pair with 20 seats.
  2. Add or remove standard tables. Removing a table sends its guests to Unassigned; Tables 1 and 7 are protected.
  3. Add guests. New guests are unconfirmed by default and appear in the Unconfirmed section.
  4. Confirm an unconfirmed guest with the Confirm button, or drag them directly to a seat to confirm and assign them.
  5. Drag guests between seats, Unassigned, and Unconfirmed. Touch devices can tap a guest and then tap the destination.
  6. Merge two standard tables to make one logical 20-seat seating area. Unmerge when no protected household spans both tables.
  7. Rename and rotate tables directly from each table header. Rotation changes the seating-layout orientation without changing assignments.
  8. Open Floor planning to enter room and table dimensions, drag physical tables, rotate them independently in 45° steps, and add variable-size blocked spaces.
  9. Open Day agenda to manage a continuous timeline that always extends to at least 04:00 on the following day. Bride & Groom events, main schedule events, and music moments use separate calendar columns in that order. Drag blocks vertically in five-minute steps. Clicking the music icon opens Spotify in a new browser tab.
  10. Export JSON saves the entire editable plan, including tables, guests, confirmation status, seats, and merges, floor positions, room dimensions, and blocked spaces. Import JSON restores it.
  11. The app autosaves in this browser. Reset returns to the original plan included in the app.
